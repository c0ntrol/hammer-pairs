Hammer Pairs

Hammer Pairs is a card game in the context of Hammers. All the cards are laid face down on a surface and the player flips two cards at each turn. The objective of the game is to find matching pairs of cards. Hammer Pairs connects the cards to the digital dataset from the Stadtarchäologie Gifhorn, creating common ground in a fun way. Once played, the amazing artworks will remain in people's minds 1 1
